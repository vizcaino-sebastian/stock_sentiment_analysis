ShipTracks Horizon is a software solution we developed in response to customer requests for high resolution tracking in remote areas. The Horizon provides a low-cost solution for offshore and remote inland vessel tracking.

How does it work? ShipTracks Horizon software turns your internet-enabled vessel into a mobile AIS receiver, recording AIS data from all vessels within range of an onboard receiver. The data is automatically forwardedto the ShipTracks network so you can view your asset and surrounding vessels on the ShipTracks display. You control the frequency of data uploads in order to save bandwidth. All reports collected by the Horizon are stored for 30 days, giving you control of data in the event of an incident.

With ShipTracks Horizon, you can keep a close watch on your operations, anywhere they are.
